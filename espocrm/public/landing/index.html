Questions, answered

A platform you can make your own

Nexa is designed for organizations that want modern customer operations with control over their workflows and data.

Can we customize records, layouts and processes?

Yes. Fields, relationships, layouts, dashboards, roles and business processes can be shaped around your operating model.

Can Nexa connect to our existing tools?

Yes. REST APIs, webhooks and dedicated connectors support communication, advertising, analytics and data-platform integrations.

Where is customer data stored?

The platform can be self-hosted so your organization controls its infrastructure, database, backups and regional data location.

Can plans be tailored for higher volume?

Yes. Email, event, reporting, workflow and integration capacity can be configured for larger operating requirements.
